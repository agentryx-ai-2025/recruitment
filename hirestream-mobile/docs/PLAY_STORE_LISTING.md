# HireStream Mobile — Play Store Listing

## App Name
HireStream – HPSEDC Overseas Jobs

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Official HPSEDC overseas job portal. Find, apply & track verified international jobs.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

HireStream is the official mobile application of the Himachal Pradesh State Electronics Development Corporation (HPSEDC) for overseas employment services. Browse verified international job opportunities, apply with a single tap, and track your application journey — all from your phone.

🌏 DISCOVER GLOBAL OPPORTUNITIES
• Browse hundreds of verified overseas job listings across 12+ countries
• Filter by country, skill category, salary range, and experience level
• Search jobs by keyword with instant results
• Jobs available in IT, Healthcare, Engineering, Construction, Hospitality, and more

✅ SAFE & VERIFIED RECRUITMENT
• Every employer and recruitment agency is verified by HPSEDC
• Government-backed placement services you can trust
• No hidden fees or unauthorized agents
• Data protected with enterprise-grade security

📱 APPLY IN ONE TAP
• Complete your profile once, apply to unlimited positions
• Upload documents (passport, certificates, resume) directly from your phone
• Get real-time application status updates
• Track every stage of your recruitment journey

🔔 STAY INFORMED
• Receive instant notifications on application status changes
• Interview invitations and scheduling updates
• New job alerts matching your preferences
• Never miss an important update

👤 COMPLETE PROFILE MANAGEMENT
• Build your professional profile with education and work history
• Upload and manage documents securely
• Set job preferences for personalized recommendations
• Edit your information anytime, anywhere

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
• Secure login with email/password authentication
• Two-factor authentication support
• Data encrypted in transit and at rest
• Account deletion available per GDPR/IT Act compliance

Built and maintained by HPSEDC for the Government of Himachal Pradesh.

## Data Safety Questionnaire

### Data collected:
- **Name**: Used for profile and job applications
- **Email**: Used for authentication and notifications
- **Phone number**: Used for employer contact
- **Documents**: Uploaded voluntarily for job applications (passport, certificates)
- **Device ID**: Used for push notifications

### Data shared with third parties:
- Application data shared with employers/agencies for recruitment purposes only

### Data security:
- Data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Data can be deleted on user request (in-app account deletion)
