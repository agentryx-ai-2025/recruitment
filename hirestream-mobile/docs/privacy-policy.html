HireStream Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

HireStream ("the App") is operated by the Himachal Pradesh State Electronics Development Corporation (HPSEDC). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, phone number when you create an account.
  • Profile Data: Education history, work experience, skills, and preferences you voluntarily provide.
  • Documents: Passport copies, certificates, and other documents you upload for job applications.
  • Application Data: Records of jobs you apply to and application status history.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate overseas job applications and recruitment processes.
  • To communicate application status updates and interview invitations.
  • To verify your identity and eligibility for positions.
  • To share your profile and documents with verified employers/agencies for recruitment purposes.
  • To send push notifications about your applications and new opportunities.
  • To improve our services and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Verified Employers/Agencies: Your application data and documents are shared with employers when you apply for a position.
  • Government Authorities: As required by Indian law or Himachal Pradesh state regulations.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS).
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device.
  • Server-side data is protected with industry-standard security measures.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data through the App's Profile section.
  • Correction: You can edit your profile information at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account through Settings > Delete Account. This removes all your personal data.
  • Withdrawal: You can withdraw job applications at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your account. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Application records may be retained for regulatory compliance for up to 3 years.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the App's notification system.
