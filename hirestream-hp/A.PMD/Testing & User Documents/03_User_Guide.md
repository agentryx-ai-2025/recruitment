# HireStream — User Guide

**Portal:** HPSEDC Overseas Placement Portal  
**URL:** https://hirestream.osipl.dev  
**Version:** Beta 0.5  
**Date:** 13 Apr 2026  
**Audience:** Candidates, Recruitment Agencies, Employers

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 What is HireStream?

HireStream is the official Government of Himachal Pradesh portal for overseas employment opportunities. It connects job seekers with verified international recruitment agencies, ensuring safe, regulated overseas placement under HPSEDC oversight.

### 1.2 Who Can Use It?

| Role | Who | What You Can Do |
|------|-----|----------------|
| **Candidate** | Job seekers from Himachal Pradesh | Build profile, search jobs, apply, track applications |
| **Recruitment Agency** | Licensed agencies | Post jobs, manage drives, interview candidates |
| **Employer** | International companies | Post jobs, review candidates, hire directly |
| **Admin** | HPSEDC officers | Verify agencies, monitor portal, generate reports |

### 1.3 System Requirements

- Modern web browser: Chrome 90+, Firefox 90+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+
- Internet connection
- JavaScript enabled
- Mobile devices: responsive design works on phones and tablets

## 2. Registration & Login

### 2.1 Creating an Account

1. Visit https://hirestream.osipl.dev
2. Click **"Get Started"** or navigate to the login page
3. Select the **"Create Account"** tab
4. Choose your role:
   - **Job Seeker** — for candidates looking for overseas employment
   - **Recruitment Agency** — for licensed recruitment agencies
   - **Employer** — for companies hiring internationally
5. Fill in your full name, email address, and password (minimum 6 characters)
6. Click **"Create Account"**
7. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

### 2.2 Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You will be redirected to your role-specific dashboard

### 2.3 Forgot Password

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot password?"**
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for the password reset link
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Log in with your new password

## 3. For Candidates (Job Seekers)

### 3.1 Your Dashboard

After logging in, you will see:
- **Profile header** with your name, location, and skills
- **Quick stats**: applications count, shortlisted count, average match score, documents uploaded
- **Job search board**: browse and apply for jobs
- **Application tracker**: see the status of your applications
- **Profile completion**: checklist showing what's filled and what's missing
- **Education & Experience**: your academic and work history
- **Documents**: uploaded CVs, certificates, passport copies

### 3.2 Completing Your Profile

A complete profile increases your match scores and visibility to recruiters.

**8 checkpoints for 100% completion:**
1. Full name
2. Email address
3. Phone number
4. Location (city/district)
5. Skills (at least 1)
6. Education (at least 1 record)
7. Work experience (at least 1 record)
8. Documents (at least 1 uploaded)

**To edit your profile:**
1. Click the **"Edit Profile"** button on your dashboard
2. Fill in or update your details
3. Click **Save**

### 3.3 Adding Education

1. Navigate to the Education section on your dashboard
2. Click **"Add Education"**
3. Fill in: degree, institution name, year of passing, percentage/grade
4. Click **Save**
5. You can add multiple education records
6. Click edit or delete on any record to modify

### 3.4 Adding Work Experience

1. Navigate to the Experience section on your dashboard
2. Click **"Add Experience"**
3. Fill in: company name, job role, years, country, description
4. Click **Save**
5. You can add multiple experience records

### 3.5 Uploading Documents

Accepted formats: **PDF, JPG, PNG** (maximum 5 MB each)

1. Navigate to the Documents section
2. Click **Upload** and select a file from your device
3. Choose document type: **CV**, **Passport**, **Certificate**, or **Other**
4. Your document appears in the list after upload
5. Click **Download** to retrieve your uploaded file
6. Click **Delete** to remove a document

### 3.6 Exporting Your Profile as PDF

1. Click **"Export PDF"** on your dashboard
2. A formatted document opens showing your complete profile
3. Use your browser's **Print** function to save as PDF or print

### 3.7 Searching for Jobs

1. Browse the **Job Search Board** on your dashboard
2. Use the search box to find jobs by keyword (title, company, skills)
3. Filter by country, experience level, or salary range
4. Click on a job to view full details
5. Click **"Apply"** to submit your application

### 3.8 Understanding Your Match Score

When you apply for a job, the system calculates a match score (0-100%):

| Component | Weight | How It's Calculated |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| **Skills** | 50% | How many of the job's required skills you have |
| **Experience** | 30% | Your years of experience vs. what's required |
| **Country Preference** | 20% | Whether the job's country is in your preferred list |

**Example:** Job requires React + Node.js + 2 years + UAE. You have React + Node.js + 3 years + prefer UAE → Score: **100%**

### 3.9 Tracking Your Applications

Your Application Tracker shows each application with its current status:

```
Submitted → Reviewed → Shortlisted → Interview Scheduled → Selected → Placed
                                                          → Rejected
```

You receive a notification at each status change.

### 3.10 Recommendations

The **"Recommended For You"** section shows the top 10 best-matching jobs you haven't applied to yet, sorted by match score.

## 4. For Recruitment Agencies

### 4.1 Agency Registration

1. Register with the "Recruitment Agency" role
2. On your dashboard, fill in the **Agency Registration** form:
   - Agency name
   - License number
   - Specializations (e.g., IT, Healthcare, Construction)
3. Submit the form
4. Your agency will appear as **"Pending Verification"**
5. HPSEDC admin will review and approve your agency
6. Once verified, you can start posting jobs

### 4.2 Posting Jobs

1. Navigate to the job posting section
2. Fill in job details:
   - Title, company name, location, country
   - Salary, description, required skills
   - Experience requirement
3. Submit the job — it becomes active immediately

### 4.3 Managing Your Jobs

- **Edit**: Update job details (title, salary, description, etc.)
- **Deactivate**: Temporarily hide the job from search results
- **Close**: Permanently close the job posting
- **View Applicants**: See who applied, with their match scores

### 4.4 Reviewing Applicants

1. Click on a job → **View Applicants**
2. See all applicants sorted by match score (highest first)
3. View candidate details: name, skills, experience, location
4. Update application status: Reviewed → Shortlisted → Interview Scheduled → Selected
5. Use **Bulk Update** to change status for multiple candidates at once

---

### 4.5 Recruitment Drives

Verified agencies can organize recruitment drives:

1. Navigate to **Drives** section
2. Click **"Create Drive"** — fill in title, date, location, target roles
3. Submit — drive status is **"Pending"** (awaiting HPSEDC approval)
4. Once approved by admin, the drive appears in the public drive list
5. You can edit drives while they are still pending

### 4.6 Interview Scheduling

After shortlisting candidates:

1. Select a shortlisted candidate from your job's applicant list
2. Click **"Schedule Interview"** — set date, time, location, mode (in-person/virtual)
3. The candidate receives a notification with interview details
4. After the interview, record the result: **Selected**, **Rejected**, or **Hold**
5. "Selected" automatically updates the application status

### 4.7 Creating Placements

For selected candidates:

1. Navigate to the selected candidate's application
2. Click **"Create Placement"** — enter country, salary, start date
3. The candidate receives a **"Placement Offer"** notification
4. Candidate can **Accept** or **Decline** the offer
5. Accepted placements update the application to **"Placed"** status

## 6. Notifications

### 6.1 How Notifications Work

You receive notifications for:
- Welcome message on registration
- Application submitted confirmation
- Application status changes (reviewed, shortlisted, selected, etc.)
- Agency verification approval
- New applications on your posted jobs (for agents/employers)

### 6.2 Notification Channels

| Channel | Default | How to Change |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **In-App** | Always On | Cannot be disabled |
| **Email** | On | Toggle in notification preferences |
| **SMS** | On | Toggle in notification preferences |

### 6.3 Managing Notifications

- Click the **bell icon** to see recent notifications
- Click a notification to mark it as read
- Click **"Mark All as Read"** to clear the badge
- Go to **Notification Preferences** to enable/disable email and SMS

## 7. Support & Help

### 7.1 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I can't find my uploaded document.**  
A: Go to your dashboard → Documents section. If the file was deleted, re-upload it.

**Q: My match score seems low.**  
A: Complete your profile — add all skills, education, and experience. The score is based on how well your profile matches the job requirements.

**Q: My agency is still pending verification.**  
A: HPSEDC reviews agency registrations. Please wait for admin approval. You will receive a notification when verified.

**Q: I forgot my password.**  
A: Use the "Forgot password?" link on the login page to reset it via email.

**Q: Can I apply to the same job twice?**  
A: No. Each candidate can apply once per job. You will see "Already applied" if you try again.

### 7.2 Contact

For technical support or grievances, contact HPSEDC:
- Portal: Submit a grievance through the portal (coming soon)
- Email: support@hpsedc.hp.gov.in

## 8. Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Candidate** | A job seeker registered on the portal |
| **Recruitment Agency** | A licensed entity that facilitates overseas placement |
| **Employer** | A company that directly hires candidates |
| **HPSEDC** | Himachal Pradesh State Electronics Development Corporation — the government authority |
| **Match Score** | A percentage (0-100) showing how well a candidate matches a job |
| **Recruitment Drive** | An organized event by an agency to recruit candidates |
| **Placement** | Successful hiring of a candidate for an overseas position |
| **OTP** | One-Time Password — sent via email/SMS for verification |
| **HIM Access** | Himachal Pradesh government SSO system |
| **Aadhaar** | 12-digit Indian unique identity number |
| **DigiLocker** | Indian government digital document storage service |
